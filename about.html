About CVACE

Castro Valley Adult & Career Education (CVACE) offers a robust Medical Academy that prepares adults for careers in the healthcare field through a variety of focused training programs. Our Medical Academy includes hands-on and hybrid pathways such as the Clinical Medical Assistant program, where students learn essential clinical skills—from taking vital signs and drawing blood to using electronic health records—and prepare for state or national certification exams with the possibility of an externship for real-world experience.
